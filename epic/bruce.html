• Name: Bruce Wayne
  • Type: Power User
  • Tech: Very tech savvy with a Batphone 20, iPhone 11, iPad 7 and office desktop PC HP with 2.5 Ghz processor and 4Gb RAM.
  • Occupation: Business Man
  • User Story: Bruce has an acquired taste for craft beer that he likes to consume after work or after fighting crime in the City Of Gotham. He has distinct preferences for his beer consumption that he feels must be met in order to be satisfied. He has been a usual drinker of local Usercraft beers for 10 years. He grew up in Albuquerque and hangs out at his favorite breweries to watch sports.
  • User Interaction: Bruce will use the FIND ME BEER APP to login and set his beer preferences by type color and ALC/BTU level. Also be able to set his favorite beers he finds on the app and save them to a list. Also be able to locate the breweries that sell this beer.
  • Goals:
    1. Wants to use the FIND ME BEER APP to make a collection of his favorite beers so he can make a quick resource to get the type of beer he is feeling like experiencing at the moment. He will use the favorites tab to store his preferred favorites and each beer profile. User can tap/click add to favorites
    2. Will use FIND ME BEER APP to locate already known or liked favorite beers and use GPS Mapbox to guide him there. Beer profile will have a “Take me there” button to initiate.
    3. Will use the app to sort through local craft beers and find new beers to consume based off his preferences.
    4. User will use ranking to apply to beer and breweries. They will use a ranking system based off beer mug icons 1-5, 5 being GREAT.
