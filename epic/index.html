Find Me Beer!

Find Me Beer! is a web app that will lead users to their favorite beer! The app will will take the users preferences into account when giving them a list of suggested beers. This can include things like brewery, beer type, ABV, IBU, location and more! Users will be able to rate and write reviews on beers as well as add them to a favorite beers list. This truly is the Beer App of the future.
